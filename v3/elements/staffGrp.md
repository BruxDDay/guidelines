            <strong>Remarks</strong>
         </td>
         <td class="wovenodd-col2">
            <p>System is the more proper name for this concept (Read, p. 37-38). Bracketed staff
               groups
               may contain other bracketed or braced staff groups or single staves. See Read, p.
               35-38,
               examples p. 434, 438.
            </p>
